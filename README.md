# Tata Group Data Analytics Virtual Experience Program

This repository contains the Power BI dashboard project completed during the Tata Group Data Analytics & Visualization Virtual Experience Program hosted on Forage.

## Project Overview

The project focused on analyzing business sales performance, customer revenue trends, and country-wise demand patterns using Power BI. The analysis involved cleaning and transforming raw data using Power Query and creating executive-level dashboards to communicate actionable business insights.

---

# Business Questions Solved

## 1. Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis

Analyzed monthly revenue performance to identify seasonal sales patterns and revenue spikes.

### Key Insights
- Revenue peaked at **1.51M in November**
- Lowest revenue observed in **February (0.52M)**
- Revenue increased consistently from **August to November**
- Q4 generated the strongest overall business performance

### Business Impact
- Identified high-performing sales periods for inventory planning
- Helped forecast seasonal demand and revenue growth opportunities

---

## 2. Country-wise Revenue & Quantity Analysis

Compared product quantity sold and revenue generated across countries.

### Key Insights
- **Netherlands** generated the highest revenue at **277K**
- **EIRE** closely followed with **273K revenue**
- Germany and France contributed over **200K revenue**
- European countries dominated total sales performance

### Business Impact
- Helped identify top-performing geographic markets
- Supported regional sales and marketing prioritization

---

## 3. Top Customer Revenue Analysis

Performed customer-level revenue analysis to identify high-value customers.

### Key Insights
- Top customer generated **272K revenue**
- Top 4 customers each contributed over **160K revenue**
- Revenue concentration highlighted importance of premium customers

### Business Impact
- Supported customer retention and loyalty strategies
- Helped identify high-value customer segments for targeted engagement

---

## 4. Global Quantity Distribution Analysis

Visualized product demand across countries using geospatial mapping.

### Key Insights
- Strong demand concentration across Europe
- Significant product movement observed in Netherlands, UK, France, and Germany
- International distribution patterns highlighted global customer reach

### Business Impact
- Improved visibility into international sales distribution
- Supported supply chain and logistics planning decisions
